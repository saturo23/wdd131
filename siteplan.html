Ubuntu Pathways: Website Planning Document

Helping African youth find opportunities, learn skills, and grow.

Site Name

Ubuntu Pathways

The name is inspired by the African philosophy of Ubuntu, meaning “I am because we are.” It represents collaboration, growth, and opportunities for youth. This site is dedicated to empowering African youth by connecting them to jobs, skills, and inspiring stories.

Optional domain: ubuntupathways.org

Site Purpose

Ubuntu Pathways aims to empower African youth by providing access to entry-level jobs, internships, volunteer opportunities, and skill-building resources. The site will also feature inspirational stories and articles to help youth learn, grow, and connect with mentors and organizations across Africa.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find internships or volunteer opportunities near me?
  • What free or affordable resources can I use to learn new skills?
  • How can I connect with organizations that support youth employment?

Color Schema

The color scheme is inspired by African earth tones and the theme of growth:

  • #2C5F2D (Forest Green) – for headings, accents, and navigation.
  • #F4A300 (Gold/Orange) – for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action elements.
  • #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) – for background sections.

Typography

The following fonts will be used for the site:

  • Poppins – for headings and navigation links (modern, bold, and clean).
  • Montserrat – for body text and descriptions (readable and professional).
