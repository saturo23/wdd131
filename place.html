Matobo Hills

Welcome to Matobo

The Matobo Hills, near Bulawayo, are a UNESCO World Heritage site known for their dramatic granite rock formations, ancient San rock art, and cultural significance. Visitors can explore caves, game parks, and historical landmarks in this breathtaking landscape.
