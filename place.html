Explore the "Red Island"

Unique wildlife, baobabs, and dramatic landscapes — a photographer and nature lover's dream.

Overview

Madagascar is an island nation in the Indian Ocean, famed for its biodiversity and landscapes ranging from rainforests to spiny deserts and white-sand beaches.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Antananarivo
  • Language: Malagasy & French
  • Population: ~28 million
